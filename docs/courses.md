## 📊 Learning Recommendations

### 🎯 Recommended Learning Paths

**Complete Learning** (for systematic improvement):
```
Lesson 0 → Lesson 1 → Lesson 2 → Lesson 3 → Lesson 4 → Lesson 5 → Lesson 6 → Lesson 7
```

**Quick Start** (for rapid onboarding):
```
Lesson 0 → Lesson 1 → Lesson 2 → Lesson 7
```

**Role-Based Customization**:

- **Product Managers**: Lesson 0 → Lesson 1 → Lesson 4 → Lesson 3 → Lesson 7
- **Operations/Marketing**: Lesson 0 → Lesson 1 → Lesson 2 → Lesson 3 → Lesson 7
- **HR/Admin**: Lesson 0 → Lesson 1 → Lesson 2 → Lesson 5 → Lesson 7

### ⏱️ Study Schedule Options

**Intensive Study** (2 weeks to complete):
- 4 lessons per week
- 2 hours per lesson
- 8 hours weekly commitment

**Moderate Pace** (1 month to complete):
- 2 lessons per week
- 2 hours per lesson
- 4 hours weekly commitment

**Casual Study** (2 months to complete):
- 1 lesson per week
- 2 hours per lesson
- 2 hours weekly commitment

### 💡 Study Tips

1. **Learn by doing**: Each lesson includes hands-on exercises, complete them on the same day
2. **Build your notes**: Record your prompt templates and usage insights
3. **Share and discuss**: Share learning outcomes with your team and learn from each other
4. **Continuous practice**: Keep applying and optimizing in your actual work after completion

---

## 🎓 Learning Outcomes

After completing all courses, you will be able to:

✅ **Cognitive Level**
- Build a correct AI mental model
- Understand AI's capabilities and limitations
- Develop learning abilities for the AI era

✅ **Skill Level**
- Write high-quality prompts
- Complete daily work tasks with AI
- Design and build automated workflows
- Select and combine AI tools effectively

✅ **Practical Level**
- Build a prompt library for your role
- Complete at least 3 real work tasks
- Improve work efficiency by 30% or more

✅ **Awareness Level**
- Develop data security awareness
- Understand AI ethics and social responsibility
- Use AI responsibly
