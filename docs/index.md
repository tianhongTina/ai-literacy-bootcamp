## Who Is This For

| Role | Core Pain Points | AI Empowerment Direction |
|------|------------------|--------------------------|
| **Product Manager** | Time-consuming requirement docs, tedious competitive analysis | PRD generation, user stories, prototype descriptions |
| **Operations** | High volume content production, time-consuming data reports | Batch copy generation, data interpretation |
| **Marketing** | Creative burnout, multi-channel adaptation | Marketing copy, multi-format conversion |
| **HR** | Writing job descriptions, creating training materials | Job descriptions, interview question bank |
| **Administration** | Meeting minutes, announcements | Document organization, formatting standards |
| **Sales** | Proposal writing, client communication | Proposal generation, script optimization |

**Prerequisites**:

- ✅ Able to use Office/WPS, browsers, and basic collaboration software
- ✅ Interested in AI but don't know how to use it systematically
- ❌ **No** programming background required
- ❌ **No** technical background required

## Learning Outcomes

After completing the training, participants will be able to:

**Skill Metrics**

- ✅ Write **5 reusable prompt templates** for their role
- ✅ Identify **factual errors** in AI outputs
- ✅ Independently use AI to complete **1 real work task** (with at least 30% time reduction)
- ✅ List **3 types of information that should never be input to AI**

**Mindset Shift**

- Transform from "Can AI do this?" to "How can I make AI do this better?"
- Proactively share AI usage experience, fostering team-wide adoption
- When encountering new scenarios, think "Can AI be used for this?"
